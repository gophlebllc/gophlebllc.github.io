Terms and Conditions

Most insurance companies do not pay for mobile phlebotomy services, therefore payment must be rendered at the time of service. However, you can submit a copy of your insurance card in order to pay for the laboratory testing fees. All specimens obtained by Go Phleb are tested by a licensed approved laboratory and results are submitted to the ordering physician.


Go Phleb will not collect any specimens without a physician order. We accept Visa, MasterCard, debit cards and cash. Service charges vary depending on the distance of your location. Discounted rates will be given to those in need of continuous services.
